My Autobiography

My name is Aprilyn Labra. I was born on April 12, 2005 in Sirao, Cebu City, and I have always been passionate about everything that catches my interest. Over the years, I have learned many valuable lessons that shaped who I am today.

I finished my primary education at Guba Elementary School, and secondary education at Guba National High School. Currently, I am pursuing Bachelor of Science in Information Systems at Cebu Technological University and have always been eager to learn new things.During my free time, I enjoy listening to music, reading books, watching K-dramas, traveling and playing different kinds of sports.
